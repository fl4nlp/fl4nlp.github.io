Data and Tasks

The training data will include the training and validation split from MultiDoc2Dial. Given the low-resource nature of MultiDoc2Dial, we do enourage the teams to explore data augmentations for tasks in two ways: (1) utilizing existing public datasets; (2) utilizing automatically generated synthetic data without involving any additional human labeled data on MultiDoc2Dial. The test data will be derived from MultiDoc2Dial dataset including multiple seen domains and an unseen domain.

There will be two subtasks. One is focused on identifying the grounding evidence of next agent response, the other is focused on generating next agent response in natural language.

Evaluations

The output of the models/systems will be evaluated based on both automatic metrics and human evaluations.
